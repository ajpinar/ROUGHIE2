MAIN BOARD (CntrlBoard)

1. Change 3 position headers (2mm) to locking type. These headers are P7 thru P11 and used for analog inputs with 5V and GND connections.

2. Add cutouts for Arduino’s USB and power input connectors.

3. Add cutout for Arduino’s ICSP pins.

4. Move traces for the DC motor connectors (P4 & P5) to the bottom.

5. Add XBee capability.

6. Move LEDs near switch.

7. Add SD card capability.

8. Add input current sensor.

9. Labels near LEDs.
